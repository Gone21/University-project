The University of Library Science and Information Technology (UniBIT) is a state university, transformed on 29.09.2010 by a decision of the National Assembly and is the successor of the Specialized Higher School of Library Science and Information Technology, voted by the National Assembly in 2004, on The State Librarian Institute, established in 1950, the Semi-Higher Institute of Library Science (PIBD), the College of Library Science (KBD) and the College of Library Science and Information Technology (KBIT).
UniBIT teaches bachelor's, master's and doctoral programs for the educational and scientific degrees for which it has accreditation.
